About Our Village

Discover the rich heritage and natural splendor of Oorakam Kunnath

A Timeless Heritage

Nestled in the heart of Kerala, Oorakam Kunnath Village has stood as a testament to the rich cultural heritage of God's Own Country for centuries. Founded in the early 1800s, our village has preserved its traditional charm while embracing sustainable progress.

The village derives its name from "Oorakam" meaning "heart of the settlement" and "Kunnath" referring to the hillside location, perfectly capturing our geographical and cultural significance in the region.
